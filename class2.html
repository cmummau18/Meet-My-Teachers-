Course Description:

The reason for taking this course was to familiarize myself with the advising tools in the Department of Communication (such as the Advising Center, Graduation Worksheets, DARS, and Pathway Planner) and the comm.-associated curricular, co-curricular, and extra-curricular resources available to me on the VT campus (including the VT Library and Career Services Office). This allowed me to meet new people in the communications department that will help me schedule classes in the future. Scout has been a huge help and I couldn't have asked for a better advisor to start my time off in the department.
