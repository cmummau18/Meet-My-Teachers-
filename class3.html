Course Description:

In this housing course we learned an introduction to the many aspects of residential housing. This course is going to be beneficial to me when I get out into the real world. We learned a lot about mortgages and how to calculate how much you can afford to pay for housing. Also, we learned about different architectural styles of homes and the pros & cons of each. The information we covered may fit into both my professional career as well as my personal life in the near future. This is the last class that I have to take in order to fulfill my Property Management Minor.

Research Interests:

Ms. Wimmer is a teacher in the AHRM department here at Virginia Tech. Her research is in textiles and how they have evolved over the years. She just oversees this housing course, but her real passion is in fashion. She's been a teacher both at Virginia Tech and at Radford.
