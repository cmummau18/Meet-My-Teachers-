Course Description:

Research is the process by which we generate knowledge about our world. This course exposed me to foundational qualitative and quantitative research methods, and enables me to gain basic proficiency in each. I have completed a range of assignments designed to prepare me for my remaining undergraduate coursework, and provides me with skills that are relevant to employers. Additionally, I have begun to learn how to read and identify the components of industry and academic research articles. Once I complete this course, I will possess an understanding of both research processes and procedures.

Research Interests:

Dr. Haenschen's expertise includes...

  • Data Journalism
  • Digital Media Influence
  • Political Participation

She has published an article titled, "Haenschen, K. "Social Pressure on Social Media: Using Facebook Status Updates To Increase Voter Turnout." Journal of Communication, Vol. 66, no. 4, 2008. She also enjoys learning new software provided online that will allow her to collect copious amounts of data to further support her research questions.
