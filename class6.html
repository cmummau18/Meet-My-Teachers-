Course Description:

The purpose of this course was to introduce me to concepts and skills that are needed to be successful as a creator of media, journalism, public relations, and other communication artifacts. In order to achieve this goal, I completed various visual and written assignments. Additionally, I was expected to become a critical consumer of media so that my own media productions were creative, ethical and understandable.

Here are a few specific items that I've learned while taking this course:

  • How to create effective communication using standard media formats and platforms
  • Basic visual design skills for layouts, photography, websites and video
  • Working knowledge of the Adobe Creative Cloud suite of media software
  • Basic knowledge of Atom
